Henry-Talent

Visit on Github: Henry-Talent

Henry-Talent was the most challenging project I have done in the Bootcamp. It was in groups of 8 and the objective was to help IT Recruiters to find the best candidates in soyHenry to hire them. What motivated us about this project was that in the future other people will continue it because was very extensive. On the other hand, we were pleased for the reason that this will help our Bootcamp buddies to find a job.
